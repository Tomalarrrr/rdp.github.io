Mosaic Mentor

Founded by HRH The Prince of Wales in 2007, Mosaic's mentoring programmes create opportunities for young people growing up in our most deprived communities. Mosiac's vision is for all young people to be supported to realise their potential by boosting confidence, self-efficacy and long term employability.

Initially taking a group of Year 11 students through the Enterprise Challenge, Phil's now completed 2 years of Secondary School mentoring at Hanson School and 1 year at Dixon Kings.
